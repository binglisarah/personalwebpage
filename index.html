Hi, I'm Bing Li!

I'm currently a postdoctoral research fellow with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University, Durham, NC, USA. received the B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Minzu University of China, Beijing, China in 2010 and her Ph.D. degree in Computer Architecture from Institute of Computing Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2016. Her research interests include computer architecture, neural network acceleration, and neuromorphic computing systems.

Research Interests:

  • Architecture and System support for emerging machine learning and big data applications
  • Emerging non-volatile memory
  • Heterogeneous computing, energy-efficient computing and edge computing
